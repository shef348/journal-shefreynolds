## Objective

Read at least 52 books - whether they are story, poetry, essay collection. Novels, or non-fiction in the calendar year. 

## Hypothesis

Since my preteens, I have consistently read between 30 and 80 books a year. In 2024, I barely read a dozen. Reading has always been my preferred leisure activity, and something I find both gratifying and fulfilling.  In 2025 I’ll get back on track by averaging a book a week, and because of the sheer quantity, I’ll be forced to carve out evening time and read a books of varying theme, medium, and length. 

## Experiment Design

Read regularly.  Log finished books in Goodreads and my journal with a 1-5 star ranking.
