## Objective

Take a daily selfie to log change throughout the year

## Hypothesis

Another low lift marker of personal growth. This is a habit that I maintained through 2023 and want to pick back up as a way to measure physical change as I age

## Experiment Design

Quick selfie in the mirror each morning. I’ll use a cheap tripod setup and keep the location consistent when at home.  On the road, as long as I remember to take a photo, I’m happy.
