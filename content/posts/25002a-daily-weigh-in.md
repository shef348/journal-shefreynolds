## Objective

Track my weight daily as a basic health marker 

## Hypothesis

Over the past 4 years my weight has fluctuated as wildly, ranging from 238lbs down to 183lbs.  By measuring my weight daily, trends will be more easily discernible over time, and I can correlate both positive and negative behaviors with weekly and monthly rends.    

## Experiment Design

Stand on scale each morning immediately after waking up.  Log in the Macrofactor app.
