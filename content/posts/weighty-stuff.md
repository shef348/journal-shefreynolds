You know that feeling when you add a bit too much weight on the bar? I wanted to capture that sensation while also not crushing myself.

I'm fortunate enough to have a garage gym, and I knew I wanted to do something with the 3x3 posts, safeties, and other lines for this challenge. Going underneath the bar felt like the logical choice.
