## Objective

Complete the [52 Frames photography challenge](https://52frames.com/) weekly through 2025. 

## Hypothesis

I love photography.  But I have done little more than snapshots since my first son was born 6 and a half years ago. By having a weekly focused challenge, I’ll commit to taking at least one photograph a week that involves focused   

## Experiment Design

Join the 52 frames community, and prioritize taking the photo of the week.  I expect this is something that will primarily done on weekends, and oftentimes when we are on the road camping. The goal here is not to take the best possible picture, but rather put some thought and time into the production of a photograph each week for a year.
